Yam and Eggs

Picture of yam and eggs

Nigerian Yam, with spicy eggs

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the yam into smaller pieces
  2. Boil yam for 30 minutes
  3. Break eggs and beat them
  4. Put your sauces in the egg mix
  5. Pour a little Groundnut oit in a frying pan and put on the stove for 2minutes
  6. Pour egg mix in the hot oil and stir
  7. serve your meal in a plate and you get what you see in the image
