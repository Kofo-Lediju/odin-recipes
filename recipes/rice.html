Rice

Rice, Stew, beef and plantain

The dish above is a native Nigerian meal

It can be made by boiling rice for about 45 minutes, cut and fry the plantain for about 10 minutes

Blending tomatoes, pepper, cut onions and boil together in a pot for about 40 minutes

Dinner is served!

Ingredients

  • raw rice
  • raw beef
  • tomatoes
  • pepper
  • Groundnut oil

Steps

  1. Blend your pepper, tomtatoes and onions
  2. Per boil the rice for 20 minutes, then wash off the excess starch
  3. Fry the oil for 2 minutes, then pour your blended pepper, tomatoes and onions
  4. Dinner is ready!!
